Prawns Curry


Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms prawns
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 4 medium tomato
  • salt as required
  • water as required
  • 6 green chilli
  • 4 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 bunch curry leaves
  • 2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 5 dry red chili
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 teaspoon tamarind

Process

  1. start by taking the grated coconut and grinding it up in a grinder. Then, add the red chilies, cumin seeds, coriander seeds into the grinder with a little water and grind it into a paste. In a large bowl, take onions and tomatoes and finely chop them up.
  2. Then, put a large pan over medium flame and heat vegetable oil in it for about 2 minutes. When the oil is hot enough, add the chopped onions in it and saute well until they are translucent. Then, add the paste along with turmeric powder and stir well.
  3. Next, add curry leaves, green chillies and salt to the pan along with the chopped tomatoes. Fry the ingredients for 8 to 10 minutes. Now, add tamarind pulp to the pan and 3 to 4 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  4. When the ingredients are done, turn down the flame to low and add the prawns. Close the lid to the pan and allow it to simmer till the prawns are tender, cook it for 5-10 minutes.
  5. When the prawns are done, transfer it to a serving bowl. Now, enjoy the delicious prawn curry with your favourite rice.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 20-30min
