Miso Soup


Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons miso paste (white or red, as per your preference)
  • 1 cup tofu, diced into small cubes
  • 2 tablespoons wakame seaweed (dried)
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms (shiitake or button mushrooms)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into thin strips (optional)

Process

  1. In a saucepan, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and add wakame seaweed. Simmer for 5 minutes until the seaweed softens.
  3. Add diced tofu and sliced mushrooms to the pot. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, dissolve miso paste in a small amount of warm water to create a smooth paste.
  5. Stir the miso paste into the soup, making sure it's well combined. Be careful not to boil the soup after adding miso, as high heat can destroy miso's flavor.
  6. Add soy sauce and sesame oil. Stir gently to combine.
  7. Remove the soup from heat. Serve hot, garnished with green onions and nori strips (if desired).


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 15-20min
