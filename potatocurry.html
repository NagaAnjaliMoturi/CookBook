Potato Curry


Ingredients

  • 2 – 3 medium size potato
  • 4 tablespoon mustard oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds (rai)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
  • ¼ teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi dana)
  • a pinch of asafoetida
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ teaspoon coriander powder (dhaniya powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dry fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 – 2 green chilli, sliced
  • Ingredients Grind into Paste:
  • 2 medium-size ripe tomato
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and chopped

Process

  1. Boil Potato
  2. To boil the potatoes use either pressure cooker or microwave. Allow them to cool down a bit. Peel and roughly dice them into chunks. Set aside till required. 
  3. Make Paste:
  4. Grind tomato and ginger into a smooth paste using water in a blender. Keep it aside. 
  5. Prepare Aloo Curry:
  6. Heat mustard oil in a kadhai till it starts smoking. Reduce the heat to low. 
  7. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, cumin, and asafoetida. Fry for 1 – 2 seconds. 
  8. Next, add the tomato paste and fry till oil separates from the masala. 
  9. Add spices, salt, and boiled potato. Stir to combine nicely. 
  10. Add approximately 2 cups of water and allow the curry to simmer over low heat till it thickens a bit. At this stage, gently mash the potatoes using the back of the ladle to thicken the curry. 
  11. Sprinkle kasuri methi, green chilli, and fresh coriander leaves. Stir nicely. 
  12. Serve Mathura Style Aloo Ki Sabzi with piping hot poori. 


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 20-30min
