Chicken Noodle Soup


Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Process

  1. In a large pot, heat the chicken broth and water over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, onion, and garlic to the pot. Simmer for about 10 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Add the shredded chicken, egg noodles, and dried thyme to the pot. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked.
  4. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chicken noodle soup!


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 30mins
