Amla Juice


Ingredients

  • ¼ cup Amla Chopped Indian gooseberry
  • 1 cup Water plus as needed
  • 1 Lemon Small, Indian variety
  • 1 to 2 tablespoon Honey
  • ⅛ teaspoon Salt I used Himalayan Pink salt

Process

  1. Take amla, cut along the lines and take out the wedges leaving the seed.
  2. Chop them roughly.
  3. Place ¼ cup chopped amla, honey, salt, lemon juice and ¼ cup water in a blender and grind in low speed until done.
  4. Strain through a metal strainer. Add remaining ¾ cup water over and strain again.
  5. Ready to serve.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 10-15min
