Vegetable Momos (Dumplings)


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Water, as needed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers), finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Process

  1. In a mixing bowl, add the all-purpose flour and gradually add water while kneading the dough. Knead until the dough is smooth and elastic. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic, ginger, and green chilies for a minute. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until they're tender. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Let the mixture cool.
  3. Divide the rested dough into small portions. Roll each portion into a thin, round disc (about 4 inches in diameter).
  4. Place a spoonful of the vegetable filling in the center of each dough disc. Fold the disc in half to form a half-moon shape. Pinch the edges to seal the momos.
  5. Arrange the momos in a greased steamer and steam for 10-12 minutes until the momos are cooked and the wrappers turn translucent.
  6. Serve hot with a dipping sauce made from soy sauce, vinegar, and red chili flakes.


Preparation Time - 45mins

Cooking Time - 10-12mins
