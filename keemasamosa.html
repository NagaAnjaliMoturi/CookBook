Keema Samosa


Ingredients

  • 1 cup ground lamb or beef
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green chilis, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Spring roll or samosa wrappers

Process

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  2. Add onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilis. Sauté for a couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add ground meat and stir-fry until it's browned and cooked through.
  5. Add the spices: ground coriander, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add frozen peas and cook for a few more minutes until they are heated through.
  7. Let the keema (minced meat mixture) cool to room temperature.
  8. Take a spring roll or samosa wrapper and place a spoonful of keema in the center.
  9. Fold the wrapper to form a triangle, sealing the edges with a little water.
  10. Deep-fry or bake the samosas until they are golden brown and crispy.
  11. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.


Preparation Time - 20mins

Cooking Time - 15-20min
