Fresh Onion Soup


Ingredients

  • 4 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 1 cup grated Gruyère cheese
  • Fresh thyme or parsley for garnish

Process

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and cook, stirring occasionally, until they are caramelized and golden brown, about 30-40 minutes.
  2. Sprinkle sugar over the caramelized onions and stir for a few minutes until the sugar is caramelized and the onions are evenly coated.
  3. Pour in the white wine and bring it to a simmer. Cook for a few minutes to reduce the wine slightly.
  4. Add the beef or vegetable broth and season with salt and pepper. Simmer the soup for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. Preheat your oven's broiler. Ladle the soup into oven-safe bowls. Place a slice of baguette on top of each bowl and sprinkle grated Gruyère cheese over the bread.
  6. Place the bowls under the broiler until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh thyme or parsley and serve hot.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 50-60min
