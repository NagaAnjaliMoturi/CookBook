Non-Veg Momos


Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken or minced meat of your choice
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 30-40 round dumpling wrappers

Process

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken (or minced meat), chopped cabbage, onions, minced garlic, minced ginger, cilantro, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well to create the filling.
  2. Take a dumpling wrapper and place a spoonful of the filling in the center of the wrapper.
  3. Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water and fold it in half to create a half-moon shape.
  4. Pleat the edges to seal the dumpling, ensuring the filling is securely enclosed.
  5. Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  6. Heat a steamer and line the steamer basket with cabbage leaves or parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  7. Arrange the momos in the steamer, ensuring they don't touch each other, and steam for 15-20 minutes or until the momos are cooked through.
  8. Serve hot with a dipping sauce of your choice.


Preparation Time - 30 mins

Cooking Time - 15-20 mins
