Mochi Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Cornstarch or potato starch, for dusting
  • Your choice of fillings (e.g., red bean paste, ice cream, fruit)

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix the glutinous rice flour and sugar. Stir in the water until the mixture is smooth.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave on high for 2-3 minutes, or until the mixture becomes translucent and sticky.
  3. Let the mochi dough cool to room temperature.
  4. Dust a clean surface with cornstarch or potato starch to prevent sticking. Roll out the mochi dough into a thin layer.
  5. Cut the mochi dough into small squares or circles. Place your desired filling in the center of each piece.
  6. Fold and pinch the edges of the mochi to seal the filling inside.
  7. Your mochi is ready to enjoy!


Preparation Time - 15 mins
Cooking Time - 2-3 mins (microwave)
