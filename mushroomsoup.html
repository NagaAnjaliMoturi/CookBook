Mushroom Soup


Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Process

  1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic and saut√© until they are soft and fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and turn brown, about 10 minutes.
  3. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup in batches to a blender, but be careful as it's hot.
  5. Return the blended soup to the pot and stir in the heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 40mins
