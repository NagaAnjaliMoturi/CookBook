Papaya Juice


Ingredients

  • 2 papayas
  • fresh lemon water
  • cold water
  • honey

Process

  1. Peel the papaya and remove the seeds. Cut the papaya into chunks or slices for easier blending.
  2.  Add the ingredients to a blender. Blend on high speed until you achieve a smooth consistency.
  3. Finally, pour in glasses and enjoy.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 10-15min

Blending - 5mins
