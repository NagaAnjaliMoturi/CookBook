Corn Cheese Balls


Ingredients

  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, frozen, or canned)
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • Oil for deep frying

Process

  1. In a food processor, pulse the corn kernels a few times to crush them slightly. You don't want a paste; you should still have some texture.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the crushed corn, grated cheese, chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to form a dough-like mixture.
  3. Take a small portion of the mixture and shape it into small balls or any desired shape.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the all-purpose flour with a little water to make a thick paste.
  5. Dip each corn cheese ball into the flour paste and roll it in bread crumbs to coat evenly.
  6. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Once the oil is hot, carefully slide the coated corn cheese balls into the hot oil. Fry in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fried corn cheese balls using a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with ketchup or a dipping sauce of your choice.


Preparation Time - 15 mins

Cooking Time - 15 mins
