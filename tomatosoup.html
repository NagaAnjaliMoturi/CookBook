Tomato Soup


Ingredients

  • 4 cups ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrots, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Croutons for garnish

Process

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions, celery, and carrots. Saut√© for about 5 minutes until they start to soften.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and the bay leaf. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove the bay leaf, and then use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth. You can also transfer it to a blender in batches.
  4. Return the soup to the pot, add vegetable broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
  5. Stir in fresh basil and heavy cream (if using). Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with croutons and additional basil leaves.


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 30-35mins
