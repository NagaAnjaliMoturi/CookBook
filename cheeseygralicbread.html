Cheesy Garlic Bread


Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Process

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the French bread in half lengthwise, creating two long pieces of bread.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, and finely chopped parsley. Mix well until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the French bread.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the buttered bread.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Place the prepared bread on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the bread is toasted to your liking.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute, and then slice it into individual servings.


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 10-15min
