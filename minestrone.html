Minestrone Soup


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup small pasta (e.g., ditalini or macaroni)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Process

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are softened.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for another 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, red kidney beans, white beans, vegetable broth, dried oregano, and dried basil. Bring the soup to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.
  5. Add the small pasta and cook until al dente, following the package instructions.
  6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 25-30mins
