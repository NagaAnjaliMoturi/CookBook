Vegetable Kebabs


Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, mushrooms, onions)
  • 1 cup paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1/2 cup thick yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons besan (chickpea flour)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Skewers for grilling

Process

  1. Begin by soaking the skewers in water for about 30 minutes to prevent them from burning on the grill.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, besan, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well to make a marinade.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and paneer to the marinade. Ensure they are well coated. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  4. Preheat your grill or barbecue to medium-high heat. Brush it with vegetable oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Thread the marinated vegetables and paneer onto the soaked skewers in an alternating pattern.
  6. Grill the kebabs for about 10-15 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are charred and cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney and naan or rice.


Preparation Time - 45mins

Cooking Time - 15-20mins
