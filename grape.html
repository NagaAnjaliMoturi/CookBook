GRAPE JUICE


Ingredients

  • 4 to 8 pounds fresh picked grapes (we use Concord grapes)
  • sugar
  • lemon juice

Process

  1. First rinse the grapes under running cold water, do rub gently while washing. Then remove the stems and overripe, soft ones. Soak the remaining grapes in water for 5-10 minutes to get rid of bacteria. (I have used organic grapes otherwise youâ€™ve to use salt and vinegar to wash to remove pesticides).
  2. ake clean grapes in a blender along with water and sugar.
  3. Blend it until smooth.
  4. Strain the juice using a sieve and discard the pulp (that has bits of skin).
  5. Now squeeze fresh lemon juice and stir.
  6. It is ready to serve.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 20-30min

Blending - 5mins
