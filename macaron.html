Macaron Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2/3 cup almond flour
  • 2 large egg whites
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Your choice of filling (e.g., ganache, jam, buttercream)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a food processor, combine confectioners' sugar and almond flour. Process until well mixed and fine in texture.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until foamy. Gradually add granulated sugar and continue to beat until stiff, glossy peaks form. If using food coloring, add it now.
  4. Gently fold the almond flour mixture into the egg white mixture until well combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a piping bag and pipe small circles onto the prepared baking sheets. Let the macarons sit for about 30 minutes to form a skin on the surface.
  6. Bake the macarons in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes. Allow them to cool completely on the baking sheets.
  7. Once cooled, sandwich the macarons with your choice of filling. Pair them up, one with a flat bottom and one with a domed top, to create the sandwiches.
  8. Your homemade macarons are ready to enjoy!


Preparation Time - 45 mins
Baking Time - 15-18 mins
