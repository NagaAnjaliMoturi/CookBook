Chicken Curry


Ingredients

  • Chicken 1kg
  • tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Lemon
  • Spice mix
  • Garam masala
  • Cilantro

Process

  1. Curry paste – Add all curry paste ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth. Set aside
  2. Pro tip – if using a small blender do this in batches. Also, make sure to cut large pieces to encourage a smooth paste.
  3. Saute paste – In a large skillet or pan over medium heat, add the oil and ground curry paste. Cook for about 2 minutes until fragrant. If necessary add a few tablespoons of water to prevent the spices from burning.
  4. Tomatoes – Next, add the chopped tomatoes and tomato paste. Cook another 2 minute
  5. Chicken – Add the chicken pieces and saute for 2 minutes more or until the chicken is well coated in all the masala. Add 1 cup water or chicken broth and combine well.
  6. Bring to a boil on medium-high heat.
  7. Simmer – Cover the pot and cook on medium heat for about 8 to 10 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
  8. Finally, add sour cream (or yogurt) and lemon juice. Simmer another 2 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning and consistency.
  9. Sprinkle with more chopped cilantro before you serve.


Preparation Time - 10mins

Cooking Time - 20-30min
