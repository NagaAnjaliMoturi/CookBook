Spring Rolls


Ingredients

  • 12-16 spring roll wrappers (also known as rice paper wrappers)
  • Warm water for soaking
  • 1 pound cooked and shredded chicken (or preferred protein)
  • 2 cups finely shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup thinly sliced carrots
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup thinly sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, or green)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Process

  1. Soak one spring roll wrapper at a time in warm water for about 10-15 seconds until it becomes soft and pliable.
  2. Lay the softened wrapper flat on a clean, damp kitchen towel or a clean surface.
  3. Place a small amount of the filling on the bottom third of the wrapper, leaving some space on the sides.
  4. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling and then roll it up from the bottom, tucking in the sides as you go.
  5. Continue rolling until the spring roll is sealed. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  6. Deep fry them


Preparation Time - 15mins

Cooking Time - 10mins
